Introduction

Train Effective is a London-based football tech company. Training app features masterclasses by Rio Ferdinand & other Premier League experts, and has over 1 Million players signed up. They also organize football camps around the world, in locations such as London, Miami and Los Angeles.

The challenge

Train Effective reach us to take their experience to the next level, make it more engaging, innovative and keep users to use the Pro Version of the app. A lot of challenges at once, but with systematic, Design driven approach - we were able to do the UX review together, suggest a new look and feel and make the application more engaging.

Train, beat your records,
get achievements

The application philosophy is centred around 4 pillars: technique, tactics, fitness, mentality. The goal is to develop players holistically and help them become not just a better football player, but more successful in life.

With training and activities, you beat your records and reach a higher level in the application. You also earn badges that others can later see in your trophy cabinet. Compete with friends and other app users.

Statistic

Keep yourself accountable by tracking your progress and reach your goals faster.

World-Class Coaching

Learn what it takes to win with the knowledge and experience of former professional players.Train Effective coaches are experts in soccer and passionate about helping you succeed just as they have. Features masterclasses by Rio Ferdinand & other Premier League experts.

Levels

Along with your activities in the application, you earn points that allow you to increase your user levels.

Compete with other community members to gain a higher position on the leaderboard. Let a higher level motivate others to be better.
