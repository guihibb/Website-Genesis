Widelab Receives Prestigious Award from Awwwards.com

In an era where the digital landscape is ever-evolving, recognition for exceptional design and innovation is paramount. As a product and brand design company, we have recently been honored with the prestigious award from Awwwards.com, marking a significant milestone in our journey of growth and evolution.

Growing up & Glowing up

For Widelab, growth has been synonymous with change. Recognizing the need to adapt to shifting markets and values of SaaS landscape, we embarked on a mission to enhance our global visibility to support more companies in their growth trajectory.

From the inception, Widelab prioritized delivering exceptional value and attracting top-tier talent. With a focus on nurturing partnerships with start-ups, we played a pivotal role in supporting their growth and success in the competitive SaaS market. Today, Widelab boasts a team of over 80 designers and developers, with a majority holding senior positions and the new website and brand need to emphasize it.

The Wide Guide: Our New Brand Story

A recent rebranding exercise allowed us to articulate our values and tone of voice authentically. By prioritizing empathy, proactiveness, and design driven culture, we ensure that our interactions with partners and team members remain genuine and meaningful.

In receiving the esteemed award from Awwwards.com, we not only celebrate our past achievements but also reaffirms its commitment to shaping the future of digital design.
